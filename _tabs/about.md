你好，我是柳耒。  
Hello, I'm Valenm.

喜欢在互联网里挖掘实用、冷门、有趣的资源，也会记录一些自己的学习笔记和折腾过程。  
I enjoy discovering practical, niche, and interesting resources on the internet, and I also document my learning notes and various tinkering processes.

这个博客主要会更新：  
This blog mainly updates:

1. 优质工具与资源整理；
   Curation of high-quality tools and resources  

2. 自己的学习过程记录；  
   Documentation of my learning progress  

3. 技术折腾笔记（博客、脚本、部署、效率工具等）；  
   Technical tinkering notes (blogs, scripts, deployment, productivity tools, etc.)

4. 偶尔的一些生活思考。  
   Occasional reflections on life

写博客的目的很简单：  
The purpose of writing this blog is simple:  

**把自己筛选、验证、并认为真正值得记录的信息整理出来，既方便日后回看，也能给他人提供参考。**  
**To organize and share information that I’ve personally filtered, tested, and found genuinely worth keeping, making it easy for me to revisit and useful for others.**

如果你愿意，也欢迎常来光临！  
If you like, you're always welcome to drop by!
