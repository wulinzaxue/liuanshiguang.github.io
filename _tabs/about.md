This blog mainly updates:

The purpose of writing this blog is simple:  

**把自己筛选、验证、并认为真正值得记录的信息整理出来，既方便日后回看，也能给他人提供参考。**  
**To organize and share information that I’ve personally filtered, tested, and found genuinely worth keeping, making it easy for me to revisit and useful for others.**
